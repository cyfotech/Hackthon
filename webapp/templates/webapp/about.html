About Community Mangrove Watch

Protecting mangrove ecosystems through community participation and technology

Our Mission

Community Mangrove Watch is dedicated to protecting and preserving mangrove forests through community-driven conservation efforts. We empower local communities, fishermen, and citizen scientists to actively monitor and report threats to these vital ecosystems.

Why Mangroves Matter

Storm Protection

Mangroves act as natural barriers against storms, protecting coastal communities from storm surges and erosion.

Carbon Storage

Mangrove forests store significant amounts of carbon, helping mitigate climate change effects.

Biodiversity

These ecosystems support diverse marine life and serve as nurseries for many fish species.

How We Help

Our platform combines modern technology with community engagement to create an effective monitoring system:

  • Geotagged photo reporting for accurate location tracking
  • AI-assisted validation to ensure report accuracy
  • Satellite data integration for comprehensive monitoring
  • Gamification to encourage community participation
  • Real-time data sharing with conservation authorities
